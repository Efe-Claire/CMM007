Landmarks

  • Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

    Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is the primary setting of the Harry Potter series, and serves as the main location for the early years of Harry's education.

  • Diagon Alley

    Diagon Alley is a fictional street and commercial area in London that is accessible to witches and wizards, and is where many of the wizarding world's most essential shops and services are located.

  • Hogsmeade Village

    Hogsmeade Village is a picturesque small village located near Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where students and adults can go to visit various magical shops and pubs.

  • Platform 9¾ at King's Cross Station

    Platform 9¾ at King's Cross Station is where students of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry catch the Hogwarts Express, a magical train that takes them to Hogwarts at the beginning of each school year.
