Landmarks in Harry Potter

Hogwarts Castle

Hogwarts Castle is a fictional castle in the Harry Potter series. It is the main setting for the first six books and serves as the primary setting for the final book as well.

Diagon Alley

Diagon Alley is a wizarding alley and shopping area located in London, England behind a pub called the Leaky Cauldron.

Platform 9 3/4

Platform 9 3/4 is a platform at King's Cross Station in London. It is used as the gateway to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Forbidden Forest

The Forbidden Forest is a large forest located on the grounds of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. It is home to many magical creatures, including centaurs, unicorns, and giant spiders.

Great Hall

The Great Hall is the main gathering area in Hogwarts Castle. It is used for meals, assemblies, and special events.

Hogsmeade Village

Hogsmeade Village is a wizarding village located near Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. It is known for its many shops and pubs, including the Three Broomsticks and Honeydukes.

Ministry of Magic

The Ministry of Magic is the governing body for the wizarding world in Britain. Its headquarters are located in London and it is responsible for regulating and enforcing wizarding laws.

Azkaban Prison

Azkaban Prison is a wizarding prison located on an island in the North Sea. It is known for its use of Dementors to guard the prisoners, who are usually Dark wizards and other dangerous criminals.

Gringotts Wizarding Bank

Gringotts Wizarding Bank is the only bank for wizards and witches in the wizarding world. It is located in Diagon Alley and is known for its high level of security, including the use of dragons to guard its vaults.
